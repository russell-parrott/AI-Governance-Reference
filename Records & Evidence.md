# Records & Evidence

[View this section on the website](https://russell-parrott.github.io/AI-Governance-Reference/records-evidence.html)

This section explains the records organisations must keep to explain how automated decisions were made.

Entries describe the types of documentation and records that allow organisations to reconstruct decisions after the fact. These may include logs, audit trails, model documentation, decision records and system documentation.

Records and evidence are essential for accountability. When decisions are challenged, organisations must be able to explain what happened, how the system behaved and who authorised its use.

This section focuses on evidence and documentation. It does not define legal obligations or organisational responsibilities, which are addressed elsewhere in the Reference.
