# Governance Tests

View this section on the website: https://russell-parrott.github.io/AI-Governance-Reference/governance-tests.html

This section presents practical tests used to examine whether automated systems are properly governed.

Entries describe the questions investigators, regulators or auditors may ask when assessing governance structures.

These tests highlight weaknesses that often remain hidden until systems are challenged.

The purpose of this section is to show how governance is evaluated in practice.
