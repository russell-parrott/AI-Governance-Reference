# Institutional Roles

View this section on the website:https://russell-parrott.github.io/AI-Governance-Reference/institutional-roles.html

This section explains the organisations and professional roles involved when automated systems are examined.

Entries describe regulators, courts, investigators, auditors and other institutions that assess automated decision-making.

When governance failures occur, these actors may investigate how systems were authorised, controlled and documented.

This section focuses on who examines automated systems, rather than how those systems operate.
