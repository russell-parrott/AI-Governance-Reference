# Legal Landscape

View this section on the website: https://russell-parrott.github.io/AI-Governance-Reference/legal-landscape.html

This section explains the broader legal environment surrounding automated systems.

Entries describe how courts, regulators and legal institutions approach automated decision-making in practice. This includes legal interpretations, enforcement trends and emerging regulatory expectations.

The aim is to help readers understand how the law is evolving as automated systems become more widely used.

This section focuses on how legal scrutiny develops in practice, rather than describing individual statutes.
