# Governance Failures

View this section on the website:https://russell-parrott.github.io/AI-Governance-Reference/governance-failures.html

This section describes common ways governance systems fail.

Entries examine structural weaknesses that allow automated systems to operate without clear authority, effective oversight or adequate documentation.

These failures frequently become visible only when decisions are challenged or investigated.

Understanding these patterns helps organisations recognise where governance systems may break down.
